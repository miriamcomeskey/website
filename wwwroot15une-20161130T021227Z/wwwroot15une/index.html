Welcome

Welcome to Kawatiri Reserve. a wonderful community project, utilising the area around the North Beach reserve.This beach area is at the Northern end of Westport.
This website will introduce you to the organisations that are setting up activities around the reserve.These organisations are all run by volunteers.


Each organisation has a page where they can let you know what is available and any activities that are coming up in the future.
Hit on the above Navigation Bar to go to a summary page of all the organisations. This page also links to all the individual pages.

Organisations

Each of these organisations has a member on the Kawatiri Reserve Committee.Along with these organisations there are co-opted members from the key businesses around the district.

Hit on the Navigation bar to find out information about these businesses.
