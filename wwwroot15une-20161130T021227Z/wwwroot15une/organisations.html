Groups

Many organisations use the Kawatiri Reserve. This page links you to many of these organisations.

The Surf Rescue Team

The Surf Rescue team patrols the North Beach Area every Weekend during the summer months.
The are constantly looking for new recruits.They would love to hear from anyone interested.

The Mountainbike Club

Buller Cycle Club have been busy building interweving tracks around the reserve. Check out their page about their working bees, the future tracks and a lot more information.

The Harriers and Walkers Club

The Harriers and Walkers have been aiding the cycle club so that the tracks are dual purpose. Read on the Harriers page of their activities including their ramble days at the beach.

Other Users

Lots of other activites occur at this Beach Reserve. Check out the "Other Users " page to see what other clubs and organisations may be organising.
